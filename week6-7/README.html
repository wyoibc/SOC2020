Adopting Tidyverse For Your Data Analysis

During the next 2 weeks, we will focus on Tidyverse which are a powerful group of packages for data analysis. While you can do a lot with the Base-R distribution, these packages may change your life. As Hadley Wickham suggests, the packages allowed for modifying important functions in R without having to change (read: break) the base-R code.

We will dedicate two weeks to this topics because there are quite a few packages to cover. We will loosely follow Wickham and Grolemundâ€™s excellent book: R for Data Science. However, we will strive to use biological data sets to work through the individual package functions.
